Why This Site Exits

Militarization of the police. Police brutality. Civil engagement. Crime statistics. Excessive force. For most, these are just empty buzzwords. Just another passing headline. Somehow, these words have become less important and less relevant than headlines about the latest hot celebrity. Even if people are able to move past the headline, the data is messy and confusing, much less motivating.

We need this to change.

Bringing Buzzwords Home has synthesized nearly half a million critical data entries into one cohesive and accessible site. With data from the United States’s Department of Defense surplus disposal program, it will be possible to see what types and quantities of free military equipment local police departments have received from the government. Data from The Guardian will allow you to track the number of fatal encounters with the police in your own community. Accessing data from the Federal Bureau of Investigation, we can make violent and property crime statistics approachable and arresting.

At Bringing Buzzwords Home, some of today’s most important and contentious issues are presented in a meaningful manner. Data presented in a compelling form has the power to reshape how you see and understand your community. We believe that technology, data science, and data visualization has the ability to prompt an audience into action. We believe in bringing this data into the familiarity of your home and out into the foreground of change.
